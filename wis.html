Alyssa Carson

Learn about Alyssa Carson, an American student with the goal of training to become an astronaut and to be selected for future human spaceflight to Mars!

Tap on the pictures to find out more!

In 2016, Alyssa was the youngest to graduate the Advanced Possum Academy, meaning she is allowed to go to space and be an astronaut trainee!

She is one of seven ambassadors of Mars One, a mission to have human-life on Mars in 2030.

At 19 years old, Alyssa has seen 3 space shuttle launches, went to space camp 7 times and Space academy 3 times!
